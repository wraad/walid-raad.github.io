Analysis of Medical Helicopter Services in New South Wales:
Unlocking £20 Million in Annual Savings

As part of a consultancy team working for a an australian based client in emergency services. I undertook a comprehensive analysis focused on medical helicopter services in New South Wales. The overarching goal of this project was to assess whether there were significant advantages, particularly in terms of response times and clinical interventions, provided by a third-party helicopter service compared to the primary helicopter service. Through rigorous analysis employing various statistical techniques, utilizing tools such as SQL, Excel, and MapInfo (GIS software), and employing effective data visualization methods, I was able to uncover critical insights. My findings indicated that there were no substantial benefits to engaging the third-party helicopter service, resulting in potential annual savings of £20 million for the client.
